Sudoku
	It is a simple puzzle game that needs concentration and focus to complete the set of 9 3X3 blocks with suitable numbers from 1-9.
	The numbers should not repeat in either a row, a column or a block.
	Neither of the boxes should be empty.
============================================================================================
Steps
	Javascript
		Random puzzle box generation.
		Prevention of editing of the generated pattern/numbers.
		Locking of entered numbers in the range of 1-9.
		Entering numbers in empty boxes.
		Editing of entered numbers.
		Colour indication of safe and unsafe entry.
		Submiting name and count of score.


	Presentation
		Html
		Node
